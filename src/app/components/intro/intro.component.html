To promote cycling as a healthy and eco-friendly mode of transportation. We aim to create a supportive and inclusive environment for cyclists of all levels. Our mission is to make South Dhaka a more livable and bike-friendly place by encouraging more people to take up cycling and advocating for better cycling infrastructure and policies.

We believe that cycling is not only a great way to stay fit and healthy, but also a sustainable mode of transportation that can help reduce traffic congestion and pollution in our city. By promoting cycling, we aim to make a positive impact on the environment and improve the quality of life for our community.

Our initiatives include organizing regular rides, events, and workshops to encourage more people to take up cycling. We also work with local authorities and organizations to advocate for better cycling infrastructure and policies in South Dhaka.

Join us in our mission to create a healthier, more sustainable future for our city. Together, we can make a difference and create a better environment for cyclists and the community as a whole.

Join our community to enjoy exclusive benefits and events. As a member of South Dhaka Cyclists, you will have access to a variety of activities and programs, including:

  • Weekly group rides: Join us for a fun and social ride every weekend. We have different routes and distances to suit all levels of cyclists.
  • Workshops and clinics: Learn new skills and improve your cycling technique with our expert-led workshops and clinics. Topics include bike maintenance, road safety, and advanced riding techniques.
  • Community events: Participate in our community events, such as bike fairs, charity rides, and family-friendly activities. These events are a great way to meet other cyclists and get involved in our community.
  • Advocacy and outreach: Help us advocate for better cycling infrastructure and policies in South Dhaka. We work with local authorities and organizations to promote cycling and improve conditions for cyclists in our city.

As a member, you will also enjoy exclusive benefits, such as discounts at local bike shops, access to members-only events, and a subscription to our monthly newsletter. Our newsletter features updates on upcoming rides and events, cycling tips and advice, and stories from our members.

We are always looking for new members to join our community. Whether you are an experienced cyclist or just starting out, we welcome you to come ride with us and experience the joy of cycling. Together, we can make South Dhaka a better place for cyclists and create a healthier, more sustainable future for our city.

For more information about our membership and how to join, please visit our website or contact us at membership southdhakacyclists.com.
